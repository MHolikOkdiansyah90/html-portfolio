The Best Movies According to Holik

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows

Film about Detective Sherlock Holmes is on the trail of criminal mastermind Professor Moriarty, who is carrying out a string of random crimes across Europe.

Real Steel

Film In the near future, robot boxing is a top sport. A struggling ex-boxer feels he's found a champion in a discarded robot.

Into the White

Film about World War II: After an aerial battle over Norway, British and German airmen find themselves stranded in the wilderness. Finding shelter in the same cabin, they realize the only way to survive the winter is to set aside the rules of war.
